What is JUP?

The UniKL-JUP programme is 3 years Diploma Engineering at UniKL MFI, fully conducted in Japanese language, before the student pursue for another 2 years in Japan in Year 3 and Year 4, to be awarded Bachelor of Engineering from Japan Universities.

Mechanical Engineering

Mechanical Engineering is an engineering branch that combines engineering physics and mathematics principles with materials science, to design, analyze, manufacture, and maintain mechanical systems.It is one of the oldest and broadest of the engineering branches.The mechanical engineering field requires an understanding of core areas including mechanics, dynamics, thermodynamics, materials science, structural analysis, and electricity.

Electrical Engineering

Electrical engineering is an engineering discipline concerned with the study, design, and application of equipment, devices, and systems which use electricity, electronics, and electromagnetism. It emerged as an identifiable occupation in the latter half of the 19th century after commercialization of the electric telegraph, the telephone, and electrical power generation, distribution, and use.

Programme Summary

More info About JUP

By 2022, This programme have a total of 395 student. JUP 1 with 137 students, JUP 2 with 133 students, and JUP 3 with 125 students.

To be able to continue our study in Japan, we need to get CGPA 3.0 or above and passing the Japanese Language Proficiency Test(JLPT)-N3 level. We are all working hard to ensure our ticket to study in Japan is secured.

Universities Available

Sports Day

The sports day was held on October 29, 2022. All JUP students participated. All students had the opportunity to demonstrate their abilities in sports.

Paper Car Competition

The competition took place on December 11, 2021. It was a student's first semester project. A necessary criterion to win the competition is that the car must be fast and straight.

Physical Education Tournament

Joining this event is compulsory for every student as it count as their marks for assignment. Either play a game or organize an event. There are three sports: soccer, badminton and volleyball.
